Production Studio Website Redevelopment & Hosting Remediation

A business website rebuild and backend cleanup project focused on improving design, consolidating hosting, and stabilising DNS and email services.

View project View GitHub

Overview

This project involved rebuilding a company website while also resolving the hosting, DNS, and email issues tied to the previous setup.

The Director wanted a refreshed website but did not want to commit to the ongoing costs of platforms such as Wix or Squarespace, so I moved forward with a custom solution using my understanding of basic web design and AI-assisted development.

Alongside the redesign, I cleaned up the backend environment by consolidating hosting, correcting DNS records, restoring missing cPanel functionality, and implementing proper email authentication.

Role Website rebuild, DNS cleanup, hosting remediation, email setup
Type Small business website and hosting project
Focus Frontend design, DNS, SSL, cPanel, business email

Key implementation points

  • Rebuilt the company website with a custom design approach
  • Aligned the frontend with the team’s preferred red, yellow, and black branding
  • Validated visual design choices through team feedback and voting
  • Corrected DNS records, including an A record pointing to the wrong provider
  • Consolidated hosting and domain services under a single provider
  • Restored access to SSL and MX record management within cPanel
  • Set up business email hosting and shared access to the info@ mailbox
  • Implemented SPF, DKIM, and DMARC to improve email deliverability

Outcome

The result was a cleaner and more reliable web presence backed by a properly configured hosting and email environment. This project reflects a practical mix of frontend design, platform troubleshooting, and small-business infrastructure support under real-world constraints.
